ABOUT US

Welcome to Boba Boba, your one stop shop for bubble tea. We made boba accessible for enthusiasts who are tired of being told how their drink should taste. You think you need someone else to decide how much sugar you can handle? Nah. You’re in charge here.

We’re not just about boba. We’re about your boba. Want a triple-scoop of tapioca pearls swimming in a sea of matcha? Go for it. Craving a mango-strawberry explosion with popping boba so aggressive it feels like fireworks? You do you.

Started by a caffeine-fueled bubble tea fanatic, Boba Boba was born out of a dream-and probably too many boba cravings to be healthy for the wallet. We believe boba isn’t just a drink; it’s a personality test in a cup.

We're here to cheer you on, provide the tools, and maybe judge your choices just a tiny bit. (Seriously though, who puts popping boba and milk in the same cup?) Ready to make your drink? Let’s get shaken, not stirred.
